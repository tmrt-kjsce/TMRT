About The Marine Robotics Team


The Marine Robotics Team (TMRT) is a student team from K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering, Vidyavihar and that focuses on building autonomous marine systems. TMRT is formed by thirty passionate undergraduate students across four years from various departments who want to apply their knowledge to design and engineer an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV). Dr. N. R. Gilke is the faculty coordinator of TMRT. We aim to compete in various National and International competitions like NIOT SAVe, SAUVC ROBOSUB and SAUC-E and in the coming years.
